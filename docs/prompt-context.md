# Prompt Context: CCTV Drain Surveys Leeds

## Project Core
- **Service:** CCTV Drain Surveys
- **Location:** Leeds
- **Language:** UK English (British spellings only)
- **Primary Keyword:** CCTV drain surveys Leeds

## Business Model & Compliance
- **Wording:** Use "Serving Leeds and nearby areas" or "Covering Leeds".
- **Strict Prohibition:** Do NOT claim to be "Based in Leeds" or have a physical office in Leeds unless a verified address is provided. 
- **Lead Gen Style:** This is a service-area network/lead gen model. 

## Conversion Goals (Primary CTAs)
1. **Phone Call:** Immediate contact for urgent or standard surveys.
2. **Short Form Enquiry:** Basic details (Name, Phone, Email, Service Needed).

## Truth Constraints Checklist (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- [ ] No fake addresses or office locations.
- [ ] No invented staff members or team photos.
- [ ] No fake accreditations (e.g., NADC, CHAS) unless verified.
- [ ] No fake reviews or testimonials.
- [ ] No fake case studies or project numbers.
- [ ] No claims of "X years of experience" or specific "guarantees".
- [ ] No "based in Leeds" claims; use "serving Leeds".

## Brand Voice Guide
- **Tone:** Professional, reliable, and straightforward.
- **Style:** Plain English. Avoid marketing "hype", superlatives (e.g., "best", "cheapest"), or excessive emotional language.
- **Goal:** Provide clear information about the service to help the user solve a specific problem (drainage issues).
